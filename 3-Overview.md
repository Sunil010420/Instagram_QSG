# Overview

Instagram Reels is a short-form video feature that lets you create, edit, and share engaging content. You can record clips, add music, effects, text, stickers, and other creative elements before you share a Reel.

Reels help you create dynamic video content with built-in editing tools. You can publish Reels to reach your audience, share ideas, showcase creativity, and interact with other Instagram users.

This guide explains how to create, edit, and publish an Instagram Reel.

## Key features

- **Video creation:** Record clips, combine videos, and adjust clip settings.
- **Editing tools:** Add music, text, stickers, effects, filters, and captions.
- **Audio library:** Add music, sounds, and original audio.
- **Effects and filters:** Apply visual effects to customize videos.
- **Sharing options:** Publish Reels to your profile, feed, or Stories.

## Benefits

- Create engaging videos.
- Reach more people.
- Increase engagement.
- Showcase content.
