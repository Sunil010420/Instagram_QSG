## Posting a Reel

Instagram Reels lets you create and share short videos with your followers and the Instagram community. This section explains how to create, edit, and publish a Reel with the Instagram mobile app.

## Create a Reel

Create a Reel to share short videos with your followers and the Instagram community. You can record a new video or upload an existing video from your device.

To create a Reel, follow these steps:

1. In the Instagram app, select the **+ icon** in the upper-left corner.

![alt text](<Image/Instagram Home screen showing the + icon.png>)

2. Select **Reel** from the bottom menu.

![alt text](<Image/Instagram Create menu showing the Reel option.webp>)

3. On the **New Reel** screen, choose one of the following options:
   - Select **Drafts** to edit a saved Reel.
   - Select **Templates** to use a predefined Reel template.
   - Select a video from **Recents** or an album to upload an existing video.
   - Select the **Camera icon** to record a new video.
  
![alt text](<Image/Reel image.jpg>)

4. Select **Next** after you select or record a video.

The **Reel editor** opens with the selected video.

## Edit a Reel

Edit a Reel to trim video clips, add audio, apply effects, and customize content before you publish it.

To edit a Reel:

1. In the **Reel editor**, drag the timeline handles to trim the video.
2. Select an editing option:
   - **Text**: Add text to the Reel.
   - **Sticker**: Add stickers.
   - **Audio**: Add music or sound.
   - **Effects**: Apply visual effects.
   - **Photo**: Add images.
   - **Overlay**: Add media over the video.
   - **Captions**: Generate captions.
   - **Voiceover**: Record narration.
   - **Filter**: Apply color filters.
   - **Import audio**: Add audio from another source.
   - **Save**: Save the Reel as a draft.
3. After you finish editing, select the **Next** arrow in the upper-right corner.

The Reel is ready to publish.

> Note: Editing options may vary depending on your Instagram version, device, and region.

## Publish a Reel

Publish a Reel to share your video with your audience.

Before you publish, add a caption, adjust sharing settings, and configure any additional options as needed.

To publish a Reel:

1. On the **New Reel** screen, enter a caption.
2. Add hashtags, mentions, links, polls, or prompts as needed.
3. Select **Tag people** to tag accounts.
4. Select **Add products** to tag products, if available.
5. Select **Add location** to add a location.
6. Select **Rename audio** to update the audio name, if needed.
7. Turn on **Add AI label** if the Reel includes AI-generated or AI-edited content.
8. Select **Audience** to choose who can view the Reel.
9. Turn on **Trial** to test the Reel with non-followers.
10. Select **Also share on...** to share the Reel on connected platforms.
11. Select **More options** to configure additional settings.
12. Select **Share**.

The Reel is published and becomes available based on your account privacy settings.
