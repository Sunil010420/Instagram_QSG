# Frequently Asked Questions

This section answers common questions about creating and publishing Instagram Reels.

## Can I save a Reel without publishing it?

Yes. Select **Save draft** on the **New Reel** screen to save your Reel and edit it later.

## Who can view my Reel?

The audience you select and your account privacy settings determine who can view your Reel.

## Can I share my Reel on other platforms?

Yes. Select **Also share on...** on the **New Reel** screen to share your Reel on supported platforms.

## Can I edit a Reel after publishing it?

No. You cannot edit the video content after publishing a Reel. Delete the Reel and upload an updated version if you need to make changes.
