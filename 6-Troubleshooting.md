# Troubleshooting

Use this section to resolve common issues when you create or publish Instagram Reels.

## Reel option does not appear

Update the Instagram app to the latest version. Restart the app and sign in again. If the option still does not appear, check your app permissions and device settings.

## Video does not upload

Check your internet connection first. Make sure the video meets Instagram's size and format requirements. If the upload fails, try a smaller file or a different video format.

## Audio is missing or does not play

Confirm that your device volume is on. Make sure the audio file is supported and available in your region. If you use a custom audio track, try a different file.

## Editing tools are unavailable

Some editing tools appear only on supported devices or app versions. Update the app and confirm that your account supports the feature.

## Reel is not published

Check your connection and available storage. Make sure your account is not restricted and that your content follows Instagram's rules.

## Need more help

If the issue continues, contact Instagram Support or visit the Instagram Help Center.
