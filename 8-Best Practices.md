# Best Practices

Use these practices to create clear, engaging, and effective Instagram Reels.

## Plan the content

Define the purpose of the Reel before you record. Keep the message simple and focus on one main idea.

## Open with impact

Capture attention in the first few seconds. Start with a clear visual, a direct question, or a strong statement.

## Use clear audio

Choose audio that supports the message. Keep background music at a balanced level so the main voice remains easy to hear.

## Keep the video easy to follow

Use short clips, simple transitions, and readable text. Avoid overcrowding the screen with too many elements.

## Optimize for mobile viewing

Create content for vertical viewing. Keep important information within the center of the frame.

## Add a clear call to action

End the Reel with a simple action, such as follow, comment, or visit a link.

## Review before publishing

Check captions, spelling, and timing before you share the Reel. A quick review improves quality and reduces errors.
